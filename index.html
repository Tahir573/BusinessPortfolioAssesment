Core Banking Systems Features


Core banking systems refer to centralized applications implemented in a bank to manage their daily banking operations. They serve as the backbone of a bank’s IT infrastructure, enabling seamless integration of various departments and processes. From customer accounts transactions to loan management and regulatory compliance, core banking systems handle critical functions of a financial institution.
